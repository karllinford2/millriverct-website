The Mission

Preserve, protect, restore, beautify

Formed under Section 501(c)(3), the Foundation works to protect this unique stretch of waterway — from Perry’s Mill Pond downstream through upper Southport Harbor — for the public and for generations to come.

Our First Priority

Restore the Tide Mill Dam

For three centuries this dam has regulated the river’s flow into the harbor. Disrepair — worsened by the winter 2025/2026 ice floes — has dropped water levels and let sediment build. Repairing it is our most urgent task.

A Call to Join Us

Help protect this special place

We are only stewards of this estuary, holding it in trust for those who come after us. Your gift, your time, or your expertise will help restore the dam and protect the wildlife that depends on it.
